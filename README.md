# 🚀 FinTwin: Your Financial Digital Twin Simulator

**FinTwin** is a high-fidelity wealth projection dashboard designed to help users visualize their 10-year financial trajectory through a "Digital Twin" model. Tailored for the modern investor, it bridges the gap between static spreadsheets and real-world wealth generation.

### 🧠 Core Features
* **The Reality Engine:** A custom simulation engine using the **Future Value of an Ordinary Annuity** formula to project net worth with monthly compounding.
* **AI Advisor (Gemini 1.5 Pro):** An integrated AI financial consultant that analyzes your "Financial DNA" to provide personalized savings strategies and "Roast Mode" critiques.
* **Behavioral UX:** Dynamic "Financial Weather" backgrounds that change based on your savings rate and real-time "Money Leak" detectors.
* **India-Centric Logic:** Native support for **INR (Lakhs/Crores formatting)** and a curated list of Indian investment vehicles (PPF, Nifty 50, SGBs).
* **Smart Risk Profiling:** An automated algorithm that suggests investment tiers (Conservative to Aggressive) based on your 6-month emergency fund status.

### 🛠️ Tech Stack
* **Frontend:** React.js, Tailwind CSS (Styling), Recharts (Data Visualization)
* **Backend:** Node.js, Express.js
* **AI:** Google Gemini API (Vertex AI / Google AI Studio)
* **Data Persistence:** Browser LocalStorage (Ensuring privacy and instant data recovery)

### 📈 How to Use
1. **Define Your Inputs:** Enter your income, expenses, and current capital.
2. **Run Simulation:** View your "Current Path" vs. an "Optimized Twin" path.
3. **Consult the AI:** Use the AI Advisor to find "leaks" in your budget and get investment recommendations.
